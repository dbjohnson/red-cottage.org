Address: 17572 Lake Avalon Rd, Hillman MI 49746

Directions: Look for a white sign with a loon that says Johnson, and follow the long narrow drive. Eventually you’ll see this hill down to the cottage - you can take your car all the way down, but know that it’s a little tight and you won’t be able to turn around at the bottom (have to reverse out), so you might consider leaving your car up at the top of the hill.
