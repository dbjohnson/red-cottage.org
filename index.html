The Red Cottage at Lake Avalon

Come visit beautiful Lake Avalon in your own private retreat!


Season: June 15 - August 15 (weekly rentals, Saturday change‑over)


Rate: $1,500 per week (Sunday to Saturday)


Amenities:

  • 2 bedrooms: 1 queen bed, 1 bunk bed and 1 single
  • Large screened porch with lake view
  • Private dock & canoe
  • Picnic table & fire pit
  • High‑speed WiFi, smart TV and wireless stereo (Bluetooth and AirPlay)
  • Pet friendly (additional cleaning fee)

Understand before visiting:

  • There is no dishwasher
  • There is no air conditioning (fans provided)
  • There is only one bathroom
  • Lake access is via steep dirt trail without handrails or steps
  • The main parking area is a short walk (100ft) from the cottage. You can park one car right outside the cottage, but the driveway is narrow and you must reverse up a hill to leave.
  • Your hosts (Susan and Bryan) are also the cleaning crew - please leave the premises like you found them (aside from linens and garbage)!
